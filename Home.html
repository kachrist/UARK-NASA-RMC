About Us

We are the Arkansas RazorBotz, a robotics team at the University of Arkansas Fayetteville that competes each year in NASA's Robotic Mining Competition. We provide all our members with useful hands on experience in teamwork and engineering. We also maintain a presence in our community through outreach to local schools to introduce students to the fields of STEM and robotics.
