2017-2018

Design Philosophy

The primary design philosophy behind this years robot was to keep the entire design as flexible and modular as possible. The chassis and excavation accomplished this by using t-slot as the primary structure allowing the drive-train and excavation arm to slide along the rails until an optimal position and range of motion was found and then locked in place. We also choose to use 3D Prints in many different areas ranging from the wheels and electrical boxes to the internals of the excavation drum.
